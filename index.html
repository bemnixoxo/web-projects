About Ethiopian Traditional Foods

Ethiopian cuisine is known for its rich flavors, unique spices, and communal dining style. Meals are often served on injera, a soft flatbread made from teff flour. Popular dishes include spicy stews, slow-cooked meats, and vegetarian platters.

Ethiopian food culture emphasizes communal eating and sharing meals. Meals are traditionally served on a large platter.
